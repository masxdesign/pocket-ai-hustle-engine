## Recommended Starting Point

You already have a live Skool community and a first course built. Here's the recommended next move:

1. **Update your first course details** — Run `/update-context` and add your actual first course title and details to the master context. Takes 2 minutes.

2. **Build your second course** — Open `business-context/course-opportunity-map.md`, pick the idea that excites you most, then run `/build-course`.

3. **Extract assets from your first course** — Run `/extract-assets` to find prompt packs, checklists, and templates hiding in your existing course. These become quick digital products.

4. **Plan your next launch** — Once you have a second course or digital product ready, run `/plan-launch` to build the full strategy.

5. **Activate your community** — Run `/build-community` to generate 30 days of engagement prompts and a new member onboarding flow for your Skool.

## How the Context System Works

All your skills read from `business-context/master-business-context.md`. This means:
- You don't need to re-explain your business every time you use a skill
- When you update your context, every skill automatically stays aligned
- As your business evolves — new courses, new audience insights, Spanish expansion — one update keeps everything current

---

## When to Update Your Context

Use `/update-context` when:
- You've launched a new course and learned something about your audience
- Your mission or direction has shifted
- You're ready to push harder on the Spanish-speaking market
- You've added new products, offers, or revenue streams
- Something about your audience has changed or become clearer
- Your Skool community has grown and the audience profile has evolved

The updater will revise your master context, show you what changed, and tell you which skills should be refreshed.

## The Bilingual Opportunity

Pocket AI Hustle is positioned to serve both English and Spanish-speaking audiences — and most AI education brands don't. Here's how to build that into your workflow:

- When using `/repurpose-content`, always ask for Spanish versions of key posts and emails
- When using `/build-community`, request bilingual onboarding and engagement prompts
- When using `/extract-assets`, flag any prompt packs for Spanish adaptation
- When using `/plan-launch`, include a Spanish-language launch sequence for your Spanish audience
- Use `/update-context` to add notes about your Spanish-speaking audience as it grows

## Tips

- **Start with one course, one product.** You don't need to use every skill right away. Build one thing, ship it, learn from it.
- **Trust the defaults.** The system generated smart starting points based on your actual business. Refine as you go.
- **This is a living system.** Every file is meant to evolve. Don't treat the first version as final — update it when your thinking shifts.
- **The community is the business.** Your Skool community is your biggest asset. Use `/build-community` regularly to keep it active and growing.
- **When in doubt, update context.** If a skill's output feels off, your master context probably needs a small update to reflect how the business has evolved.
