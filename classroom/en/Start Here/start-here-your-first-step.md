# Your First Step

Three things. Do them in order.

---

**1. Post your intro in the community feed**

Name, situation, one thing you want to build. Five minutes. Do this first — it creates a small public commitment that makes everything after it more likely to happen.

---

**2. Open the first course and start at Lesson 1**

Go to the Classroom tab. Pick the course that fits where you are right now. Start at Lesson 1 — not the module that sounds most interesting, not the one that feels most relevant. Lesson 1.

Every course is built so the first lesson sets up everything after it. Starting anywhere else creates gaps.

---

**3. Do the action task at the end of every lesson**

Each lesson ends with a short, specific task — usually 15–30 minutes. Do it before moving on. Tick the box after. Repeat.

This one habit is the difference between finishing with something built and finishing with nothing.

---

After each module, come back to the community and share one thing — a win, a question, something you made. One sentence is enough. Staying visible keeps you moving.

There is no perfect moment to start. You are already here.

Go post your intro. Then open Lesson 1.
