# How Skool Works

Four tabs. Here is what each one does:

**Community** — the main feed. Post updates, share wins, ask questions. This is where the conversation happens.

**Classroom** — where the courses live. You are here right now. Pick a course, start at Lesson 1, work through in order.

**Members** — everyone in the community. Click any member to view their profile or send a direct message.

**About** — overview of the community, what is included, and who it is for.

## Tracking your progress

Each lesson has a tick box. Check it when you finish the lesson **and** complete the action task — not before.

This matters for a few reasons:

- It keeps your progress honest — a ticked box means you actually did something
- It shows you how far you have come on days when motivation is low
- It helps you find your place again if you step away for a few days
- It shows us where members are getting stuck so we can improve those lessons

The habit: finish the lesson → do the action task → tick the box → move on.

Do not tick until you have done both. That small discipline is what separates people who finish from people who drift.
