# Idea Generation Using AI

## What you'll learn

- How to use AI to generate and refine product ideas
- Simple prompts you can copy right now to get started
- How to filter AI-generated ideas and find your best option

## Why it matters

You do not need to wait for inspiration. AI can help you generate dozens of product ideas in minutes. Once you know how to use it for idea generation, you will never be stuck staring at a blank page wondering what to build.

## Explanation

AI is excellent at brainstorming. It does not get stuck, it does not judge your ideas, and it can generate options across different formats, audiences, and topics faster than any human.

The key is giving it the right inputs. The more specific your prompt, the more useful the output.

Think of AI as a brainstorming partner who knows a lot about digital products. You bring the topic or the audience. AI brings the ideas.

Here is what good idea generation looks like:

- You tell AI who you are creating for
- You tell AI what they struggle with
- AI generates a list of product ideas
- You pick the one that fits you best
- You use AI again to develop it further

## Step-by-step

1. Open your AI tool of choice
2. Copy and use one of the prompts below
3. Read through the ideas it generates
4. Pick two or three that feel realistic and interesting
5. Use a follow-up prompt to develop your favourite idea further

## Example

Here are three prompts you can copy right now:

---

**Prompt 1 — General idea generation**

"I want to create a simple digital product for beginners who are just starting to use AI tools. Give me 10 ideas for products I could create in one day. Each idea should solve one specific problem. Keep each idea simple enough for a beginner to create."

---

**Prompt 2 — Audience-specific ideas**

"I want to create a digital product for [freelance writers / career switchers / small business owners — choose one]. What are the five most common problems they face when starting out? For each problem, suggest one simple digital product I could create to help solve it."

---

**Prompt 3 — Format-specific ideas**

"I want to create a prompt pack for [insert your chosen audience]. Give me 10 ideas for prompt pack topics. For each idea, write one sentence describing who it is for and what problem it solves."

---

Use the output to spark your thinking. You do not need to copy the idea exactly — use it as a starting point and make it your own.

## Action task

Use Prompt 1 or Prompt 2 above with your AI tool. Write down the three ideas from the output that appeal to you most.

Then ask AI: "For idea number [X], what would a basic version of this product include? Give me a simple outline."

Write down the outline AI gives you. Save it alongside your hypothesis from Module 1.

You now have two things: a confirmed idea and a rough outline. These are Steps 1 and 2 of the AI workflow you are about to learn in Module 3. You are already further along than you think. The next module shows you how to take what you have and turn it into a finished product.

## Common mistake

Taking every AI-generated idea at face value without filtering. AI will generate ideas that sound good but do not match your skills or audience. Always ask yourself: "Can I build this? Do I actually know something about this topic?" Pick ideas that match your real experience, not just ideas that sound interesting.
